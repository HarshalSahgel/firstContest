About Vrindavan

Vrindavan, the land of Radha Krishna has about 5500 temples dedicated to them to showcase their divine pastimes.Vrindavan is important to Hinduism because it is the place where Krishna, the eighth incarnation of Vishnu, spent his childhood and performed many of his divine pastimes. Vrindavan is considered to be a holy city and a major pilgrimage site for the devotees of Krishna, especially the Gaudiya sect of Vaishnavism
